Our Story

MindCare was founded in 2018 by a team of mental health professionals and technologists who saw firsthand how difficult it was for people to access quality therapy.

Long waitlists, high costs, and inflexible scheduling created insurmountable barriers for those who needed help the most. We knew there had to be a better way.

By combining technology with compassionate care, we've built a platform that puts you in control of your mental health journey. Today, we're proud to have helped over 2 million people find the support they need to live happier, healthier lives.
