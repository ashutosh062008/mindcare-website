Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindCare

How do I get started with MindCare?

+

Getting started is simple! Just click the "Get Started" button, complete a brief questionnaire about your needs and preferences (takes about 5 minutes), and we'll match you with a licensed therapist within 24 hours. You can then schedule your first session or start messaging your therapist right away.

Are the therapists licensed and qualified?

+

Yes! All MindCare therapists are licensed, trained, and experienced in their respective fields. We require therapists to have a Master's degree or Doctorate in their field, plus at least 3 years and 2,000 hours of hands-on experience. They must also be licensed by their state board and pass a comprehensive background check.

How much does MindCare cost?

+

MindCare costs between $60-$90 per week (billed monthly), which is significantly less than traditional in-office therapy that can cost $100-$200 per session. Your subscription includes weekly 30-45 minute live sessions plus unlimited messaging with your therapist. We also offer financial aid for those who qualify.

Does insurance cover MindCare?

+

MindCare does not currently work directly with insurance companies. However, many clients are able to get partial reimbursement from their insurance. We provide you with all necessary documentation to submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Check with your insurance provider about out-of-network mental health benefits.

What if I don't like my therapist?

+

Finding the right therapist is important, and we understand it may take time. You can switch therapists at any time for freeâ€”just let us know and we'll help you find a better match. There's no limit to how many times you can switch, and it won't affect your pricing or service.

Is my information confidential?

+

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All communication between you and your therapist is completely confidential and protected by HIPAA regulations. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption to secure all your data. Your therapist is bound by professional and legal confidentiality requirements.

How does online therapy compare to in-person therapy?

+

Studies show that online therapy is just as effective as in-person therapy for most conditions. The convenience and accessibility of online therapy often leads to better consistency and outcomes. You get the same professional care, evidence-based treatment, and therapeutic relationshipâ€”just from the comfort of your own space.

What types of therapy do you offer?

+

We offer individual therapy, couples therapy, and teen therapy. Our therapists use various evidence-based approaches including CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy), DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy), psychodynamic therapy, solution-focused therapy, and more. Your therapist will work with you to determine the best approach for your needs.

Can I message my therapist between sessions?

+

Yes! Unlimited messaging is included with your subscription. You can send messages to your therapist anytime, and they'll respond within 24 hours on business days. This ongoing support between sessions is one of the unique benefits of online therapy.

What equipment do I need?

+

All you need is a device with internet connectionâ€”smartphone, tablet, or computer. For video sessions, you'll need a camera and microphone (built into most devices). For the best experience, we recommend a quiet, private space with stable internet connection.

Can I cancel my subscription?

+

Yes, you can cancel anytime with no penalties or fees. If you cancel mid-billing period, you'll have access to your therapist until the end of your current billing period. We also offer the option to pause your subscription if you need a temporary break.

Is MindCare right for me?

+

MindCare is great for people dealing with anxiety, depression, stress, relationship issues, life transitions, and many other challenges. However, we may not be suitable for severe mental health crises, substance abuse emergencies, or conditions requiring in-person medical procedures. If you're in crisis, please call 988 or go to your nearest emergency room.

How do you protect my data?

+

We use industry-leading security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and strict access controls. We're fully HIPAA compliant and never sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States.

How quickly can I start therapy?

+

You can typically start messaging your therapist within 24 hours of completing the questionnaire. Live video or phone sessions can usually be scheduled within a few days, depending on your and your therapist's availability. Many of our therapists have same-week availability.

What happens during the first session?

+

Your first session is an opportunity to get to know your therapist and discuss what brought you to therapy. Your therapist will ask about your background, current challenges, and goals. Together, you'll create a treatment plan and decide on the best approach for your needs. It's also a great time to ask any questions you have about the therapy process.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact our support team anytime.
